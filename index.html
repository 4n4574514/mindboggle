Software¶

Mindboggle’s open source, freely available python software for automated labeling and shape analysis of human brain imaging data.

Data¶

The Mindboggle-101 dataset, the largest collection of publicly available, manually labeled human brain surfaces in the world, and a variety of shape measures computed on each labeled region and for each anatomical features derived from the images (sulci, fundi).
